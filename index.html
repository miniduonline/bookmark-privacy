Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [2025/02/23]

Thank you for using Bookmark Pro. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Chrome extension.

1. Information We Collect

Our extension may collect the following information:

  • Bookmarks Data: We store information related to your saved bookmarks, including URLs, names, notes, and custom icons. This data is stored locally in your browser and is not shared with any third parties.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymized data on how the extension is used to improve performance and user experience. This may include page views, interactions with the extension, and error reports. This data is not personally identifiable.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide the functionality of the extension, such as saving and managing bookmarks.
  • To improve the user experience by analyzing how users interact with the extension.
  • To respond to user feedback and support requests.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device and is not transferred to any remote servers. The data you save (bookmarks, names, notes, and icons) is stored in your browser's local storage and can be accessed only by you.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information or bookmark data to third parties. Your data is stored locally on your device and is never shared with external services unless explicitly stated.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, please be aware that no data transmission method over the internet is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify users of any significant changes through a notice in the extension or through other appropriate communication channels.
